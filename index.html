What is Thumbnail Preview?

"Thumbnail Preview" aims to render a traditionally paid experience to users for free, granting ease and utility to editors and content creators everywhere.


What happens with my data?

Thumbnail Preview is an exclusively client-side tool, meaning your photos aren't uploaded or stored on any server. See the source code here.


Why free?

Why not? At its core, my purpose is to raise the standards, ease and accessibility of video editing. Accessibility is key to my vision for a more entertaining future. Enjoy eternally free products that enhance your edits and impress millions.


Where can I find more free editing tools?

Through the Ducky's Media website and YouTube channel, you can download, learn to create, and learn to implement dozens of free editing presets, tools and plugins.
